Content Marketing That Works

By Jeremy LaFaver | April 2025

Content marketing isn’t just a buzzword—it’s how smart businesses grow in 2025. But with so much noise online, how do you make your content stand out, rank high, and actually drive results?

1. Start with Your Audience

Who are you talking to? What do they care about? The best content solves real problems for real people. Get specific—think “Utah business owners” or “busy parents in Salt Lake City.”

2. Focus on Value, Not Just Keywords

Yes, SEO matters. But Google (and your readers) want helpful, original content. Answer questions, share tips, and give away your best advice. The more value you provide, the more people will trust—and share—your work.

3. Mix Up Your Formats

Blog posts, videos, infographics, podcasts—different people like different content. Repurpose your best ideas across formats to reach a wider audience.

4. Be Consistent

One great post is nice. A steady stream of useful content builds authority. Set a schedule you can stick to—even if it’s just once a month.

5. Measure What Matters

Track your results! Look at traffic, engagement, and conversions—not just likes. Use what you learn to improve your next piece.

Real-World Example

I worked with a local tech company to launch a series of how-to guides. Within six months, their organic traffic tripled and they started getting leads from across the state. That’s the power of content that works.

Quick Tips

  • Write for people first, search engines second.
  • Use clear headlines and short paragraphs.
  • Include real stories and examples.
  • Promote your content—don’t just publish and pray.
