Why Every Business Needs a Website in 2025

By Jeremy LaFaver | May 15, 2025

Let’s be real: in 2025, if your business doesn’t have a website, you’re missing out—big time. Whether you’re a local shop in Utah or a startup with global dreams, your website is your digital handshake, your storefront, and your best salesperson—all rolled into one.

First Impressions Matter

Think about the last time you searched for a service or product. Did you check out their website? So do your customers. A clean, modern site builds trust instantly. It shows you’re legit, professional, and ready to help.

24/7 Accessibility

Your website never sleeps. While you’re catching some Z’s, your site is answering questions, showing off your work, and even making sales. That’s business growth on autopilot.

Mobile-First is a Must

Over 60% of web traffic comes from mobile devices. If your site isn’t mobile-friendly, you’re turning away more than half your potential customers. Responsive design isn’t a luxury—it’s a necessity.

SEO: Get Found, Get Chosen

Search engines are the new Yellow Pages. With smart SEO, your business pops up when people need you most. That means more eyes, more clicks, and more customers.

Real-World Results

Here’s a quick story: I work with a local sports event Director in Salt Lake City. After launching their new website, they saw a 1500% jump in google search views alone. That’s the power of a great web presence. And that's just Google.

Quick Tips for 2025

  • Keep your design clean and easy to navigate.
  • Make sure your site loads fast—speed matters.
  • Use real photos and tell your story.
  • Update your content regularly (Google loves fresh info).
  • Don’t forget clear calls to action (like “Contact Us” or “Order Now”).
