Brand Kit: Social Spark (Social Media Management & Content Creation)
This brand kit is designed to give your Social Media Management and Content Creation product a professional, engaging, and results-oriented identity. We'll leverage your existing "Jax of All Trades" brand colors while introducing specific elements that speak to social media growth and content expertise.

1. Product Name & Tagline

Product Name: Social Spark
Tagline: Ignite Your Online Presence.
"Social" clearly defines the area, and "Spark" implies ignition, creativity, growth, and getting things started. It's energetic and memorable.
It's active, benefits-driven, and aligns with the "Spark" concept, promising visible results.

2. Brand Core

Mission: To empower businesses to build vibrant, engaging, and effective social media presences through strategic management and compelling content, ultimately driving measurable growth.
Vision: To be the trusted partner for businesses seeking to master their social media storytelling and unlock their full online potential.
Values:
  • Engagement: Fostering genuine connections with audiences.
  • Creativity: Producing captivating and original content.
  • Strategy: Data-driven approaches for optimal results.
  • Consistency: Maintaining a steady, impactful online voice.
  • Transparency: Clear reporting and communication.
Unique Selling Proposition (USP): "Social Spark goes beyond basic posting. We fuse expert social media management with high-impact content creation, ensuring your brand not only gets seen but truly connects and converts."
Target Audience:
  • Small to Medium Businesses (SMBs): Overwhelmed by social media, lack time or expertise.
  • Entrepreneurs/Solopreneurs: Need a professional online presence but don't have a dedicated marketing team.
  • Startups: Looking to build brand awareness and a community from scratch.
  • Businesses focused on online sales/leads: Need social media to drive traffic and conversions.

3. Visual Identity

Logo Concept:
  • Primary Icon: A stylized, abstract "S" or a combination of an "S" and a spark/flame/starburst. It could also incorporate subtle elements of a speech bubble or network connection.
  • Integration: This icon would sit alongside the "Social Spark" wordmark, using a font similar to or complementary to "Inter Bold" from your main brand.
  • Colors: Primarily use your Gold/Yellow accent (#FFD700 or #FFB300) as the primary color for the spark/icon, with Dark Blue (#14245C) or Dark Gray (#181818) for the text.
Color Palette (Product Specific):
  • Primary Accent: Gold/Amber (#FFB300 / #FFD700) - Carries over your existing strong accent, symbolizing energy, attention, and value.
  • Secondary Accent: A vibrant, but not overwhelming, Teal/Aqua (#00A3C9) - Represents communication, innovation, and digital connectivity. This adds a fresh, modern twist.
  • Neutrals: Leverage your existing Dark Gray (#181818) and Dark Blue (#14245C) for backgrounds, text, and supporting elements to maintain consistency with the main brand.
  • Highlight: White (#FFFFFF) for contrast and readability.
Typography:
  • Headings: Continue using Inter Bold or a similar strong, clean sans-serif for headlines (e.g., "Social Spark," "Ignite Your Online Presence").
  • Body Text: Continue using Inter Regular for readability in longer descriptions and social media captions.
  • Emphasis: Use bolding and perhaps a slightly larger size for key calls to action.
Imagery & Iconography:
  • Style: Clean, dynamic, and modern. Focus on aspirational themes and connection.
  • Themes: Connectivity, Growth, People/Community, Abstract Spark Elements.
  • Icons: Use a consistent set of line icons or flat icons that represent social media platforms, content types, and growth metrics.

4. Brand Voice & Messaging

Tone of Voice:
  • Enthusiastic & Energetic
  • Strategic & Insightful
  • Supportive & Empowering
  • Clear & Action-Oriented
Key Messages & Taglines:
  • Primary Tagline: "Social Spark: Ignite Your Online Presence."
  • "Stop scrolling, start shining. We build social strategies that captivate and convert."
  • "Your story, amplified. Expert content creation and social media management for real results."
  • "Gain followers, gain customers. Unlock consistent growth on social media."
  • Problem/Solution: "Tired of inconsistent posts and low engagement? Social Spark brings your brand to life online."

5. Brand Collateral (Product Specific)

  • Website Landing Page: A dedicated section on your website for "Social Spark" showcasing its features, benefits, case studies, and clear calls to action.
  • Social Media Profile Optimization: Cover Photos/Banners, Ad Graphics.
  • Content Templates: Social Post Templates, Blog Post Outline/Templates, Content Calendar Templates.
  • Productized Packages/Pricing Guides: Professionally designed PDFs or web pages outlining your Social Spark service tiers.
  • Case Study Templates: Focused on social media and content success stories.
  • Client Onboarding Documents: Welcome packets, questionnaires, and strategy brief templates.

6. Brand Guidelines (for Social Spark)

  • Logo Usage: Clear guidelines for the "Social Spark" logo, its icon, minimum size, and color variations.
  • Product Color Palette: Specific HEX codes for the gold/amber and teal accents, along with usage examples.
  • Typography: Which fonts to use for headlines and body text within Social Spark marketing materials.
  • Imagery Style: Examples of the type of photos and illustrations that align with the "Social Spark" brand.
  • Voice & Tone: Specific phrases or keywords to use, and those to avoid, when talking about "Social Spark."
