How to Choose the Right Logo for Your Brand

By Jeremy LaFaver | June 2025

Your logo is more than just a pretty picture—it’s the face of your brand. In 2025, a great logo can help you stand out, build trust, and tell your story at a glance. But how do you choose the right one?

1. Know Your Brand Personality

Are you bold and modern, or classic and trustworthy? Your logo should reflect your vibe. Jot down a few words that describe your business, and keep them in mind as you explore ideas.

2. Keep It Simple (But Memorable)

The best logos are easy to recognize—even at a glance. Think of the Nike swoosh or Apple’s apple. Avoid clutter and focus on one strong idea.

3. Choose Colors Wisely

Colors have power! Blue feels trustworthy, red is energetic, yellow is cheerful. Pick a palette that fits your brand and works well in black and white, too.

4. Make It Versatile

Your logo should look great on a business card, a billboard, or your website. Test it at different sizes and on different backgrounds.

5. Get Feedback (and Trust Your Gut)

Show your logo ideas to friends, customers, or even strangers. Listen to their reactions, but remember: you know your brand best.

Real-World Example

I recently helped a Utah startup rebrand with a new logo. After switching to a bold, simple mark, their social media engagement doubled—and customers started recognizing them at local events. That’s the power of a great logo!

Quick Tips

  • Don’t follow trends blindly—timeless beats trendy.
  • Make sure your logo is unique (do a quick Google search).
  • Use a professional designer if you can—it’s worth it.
  • Test your logo in real-world situations before finalizing.
