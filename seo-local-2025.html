5 Ways to Boost Your Local SEO in 2025

By Jeremy LaFaver | July 2025

Want more local customers in 2025? You need to show up where they’re searching—on Google, Maps, and beyond. Here are five proven ways to boost your local SEO and get found fast.

1. Claim and Optimize Your Google Business Profile

This is your digital storefront. Make sure your business info is accurate, add real photos, and keep your hours up to date. Ask happy customers for reviews—they matter more than ever.

2. Use Local Keywords (the Smart Way)

Think like your customers: “best pizza in Salt Lake City” or “Utah web designer.” Sprinkle these phrases naturally into your site’s content, titles, and meta descriptions.

3. Get Listed in Local Directories

Sites like Yelp, Yellow Pages, and local chamber of commerce listings help search engines trust your business. Make sure your name, address, and phone number are consistent everywhere.

4. Create Location-Specific Content

Write blog posts about local events, news, or customer stories. Show you’re part of the community. Google loves fresh, relevant content.

5. Build Local Backlinks

Partner with other local businesses, sponsor events, or get featured in local news. Every quality link to your site boosts your authority and rankings.

Real Results

I helped a local coffee shop in Utah update their Google Business Profile and add a few local blog posts. Within two months, their map views doubled and foot traffic increased by 30%. Local SEO works—if you work it!

Quick Tips

  • Respond to every review (good or bad).
  • Keep your website mobile-friendly and fast.
  • Use real photos of your team and location.
  • Update your info regularly—don’t set and forget.
